Maybe that is its own kind of truth, shy and unbuttoned. Not every river wants a bridge. Not every log line resolves into prophecy. Some days are just soft compilations of maybe.

The light this afternoon was #E8DCC4, tea-colored and forgiving. I listened to the faint hum of things continuing without explanation, and for once, it sounded almost kind.
